Falcon

[ Play on itch.io ]

Created as a base for a mobile game, but has been left on hold for the foreseeable future in pursuit of other projects. Nonetheless, it was fun to explore and iterate upon one of my first physics based projects, which have since been one of the most fun kind of games for me to work on in my career. I believe physics based gameplay combined with some sort of competitive element, whether against yourself or another player, can lead to a lot of emergent gameplay since the laws of physics are something that players intuitively understand, as well as the fact that they apply to every object in the game in the same manner, not restricting the player to unfair or unexpected restrictions with certain objects or situations. If I were to expand on this project, I would love to add enemies or breakable obstacles, then tie the amount of damage done to those entities by the player to the player's velocity. This is where the working name "Falcon" comes from, where you can imagine working to fly high up in the sky, then precisely, but while going incredibly fast, nose dive onto your prey.
