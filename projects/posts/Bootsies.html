Bootsies

[ Find Out More On Our Website! ]

Bootsies is something I'm very proud to be working on currently, with some help from great friends from FGC@UCLA! I've wanted to work on a fighting game for the longest time, but I had been waiting for the right opportunity with the right team since I can certainly admit my lack of skills in other areas besides 3D art and programming. Fighting games have been my passion since I discovered the fighting game community at UCI; I've made amazing friends through the FGC, as Bootsies is very much evidence of, and fighting games have been an amazing competitive outlet for me to challenge my mental toughness, physical aptitude under pressure, and to challenge myself and my opponents to constantly improve and reflect upon our current abilities. Bootsies is a love letter to fighting games and their communities everywhere and our team certainly hopes it shows!

So far, I've developed several 3D backgrounds for the game, in hopes to give the game a unique retro inspired look, comparable to classic games like Capcom vs. SNK 2. I've also been implementing rollback netcode for the game, so you can look forward to playing with your friends, even if they're across the country, or even in a different country entirely!
