Untitled

[ Play on itch.io ]

Another physics based demo, this time inspired by rocket jumping from Team Fortress 2! Although this project was a very short venture, once again, the physics were very enjoyable to iterate on, and I think the design of holding left-click in order to store more power to propel yourself further adds a good amount of challenge and a learning curve, even within the constraint of only having this central mechanic. In order to make it to the end of the level, you'll have to get accustomed to holding left-click just the right amount, even while falling midair, in order to propel yourself forward enough at the perfect angle. If I were to expand on this project, I would definitely add better feedback for this mechanic; even though I do think there is some fun to be had from not making it completely obvious, having no feedback at all makes the mechanic rather unfair for the player to learn to use 100% consistently.
