Yakamoto Clean Sweep

[ Download on itch.io ]

I also helped to create "Yakamoto Clean Sweep" as a part of my activities at the Game Development Club at UCI. I engineered systems and mechanics for weapons featured in the game and other gameplay aspects. One example of this would be the foundation I created for all weapons featured in the game, which the rest of the programmers would build on top of in order to finish all the unique weapons in the game. Being C#, this system was based around an abstract class for weapons, but also integrated an event based system in order for any object to be able to know about when a weapon deals damage or creates an effect, without having to even know the underlying type of the weapon. I also built off of this system myself, creating several unique weapons for the game, some even affecting your movement through physics, such as the "Soap Talisman".
